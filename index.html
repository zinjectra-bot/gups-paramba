About Our School

ഞങ്ങളുടെ സ്കൂളിനെക്കുറിച്ച്

GUPS പറമ്പ is a government primary school dedicated to providing quality education to the children of Pookkottumpadam and surrounding areas. We focus on holistic development of our students through modern teaching methods and traditional values.

Our school emphasizes both Malayalam and English languages, ensuring students are well-prepared for their future academic endeavors while staying connected to their cultural roots.

  • Qualified and experienced teachers
  • Modern classroom facilities
  • Sports and extracurricular activities
  • Computer education
  • Library and reading programs
